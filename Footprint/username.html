    Recommended safety checks

      Pattern-based identity analysis

      The checker combines multiple lightweight methods instead of relying on a single percentage. This keeps the feature suitable for a browser extension without using large-scale machine learning.

      1

      Levenshtein distance

      Counts the minimum insertions, deletions and substitutions required to transform one username into the other.

      2

      Jaro-Winkler similarity

      Measures matching characters and gives extra importance to usernames that share the same beginning.

      3

      Homograph checks

      Detects selected Greek and Cyrillic characters that visually resemble ordinary Latin letters.

      4

      Modification patterns

      Checks number swaps, separators, repeated characters, prefixes, suffixes and other common impersonation changes.

      Footprint provides risk awareness and does not automatically remove or confirm accounts.
